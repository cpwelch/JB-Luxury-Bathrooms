What we offer

We specialise in three types of electric underfloor heating due to the fact they are easier to retrospectively fit and can offer great quality for a fraction of the installation cost.


  • Overlay System
  • Spreader plate system
  • Screeded hardfloor system

Underfloor heating is an easy and energy efficient way to warm your home, and to keep your toes cosy. Although the cost of installing underfloor heating is higher than the installation cost of traditional radiators, there are various options available to suit your budget and it’s worth keeping in mind that underfloor heating provides substantial cost savings on your energy bills in the longer term.


When renovating your bathroom, underfloor heating is especially worth thinking about. You will save a lot on the labour cost if the flooring is being lifted up and changed anyway. The comfort and the cost savings of running the system on your heating bills will work out to your advantage in a longer run.
