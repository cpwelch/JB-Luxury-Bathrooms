FAQs:

What is the time scale of having a bathroom fitted?

Once the design and layout has been confirmed by all parties, the average timescale is 15 days. This is on the basis that the bathroom is an ‘average’ sized bathroom and the room is being stripped back to the brick. If the bathroom is smaller, then it could be quicker, providing there are no technical complications due to size. For larger bathrooms, the timescale is likely to be closer to 4 weeks.

What is included in the price?

In the price we include the supply, design and installation of your luxury bathroom. This includes: plumbing, joinery, tiling, plastering and decorating. We pride ourselves on being able to manage the whole process, start to finish.

Can I use the bathroom whilst you work on it?

We will always reconnect the toilet where possible and your bathroom will be left as clean and tidy as it can be when we leave. This is useful when you only have one bathroom in the house. However, we can not guarantee you will be able to shower or use the bath until completion.

Why should I choose you over other companies?

We are an experienced team with many skills that we always apply to every project we take on. As a small and local business we always strive to offer a personal and individual service to every one of our customers. We will make the whole process - from initial conversation through to design and completion as hassle free as possible. We conduct our work in a professional manner, respecting your home whilst we work within it and take the necessary precautions to ensure you are left with a completely transformed room and house to be proud of.

Do I need any planning permission for my new luxury bathroom?

Usually there is no need for planning permission for fitting a new bathroom unless the building is a listed building. There may however be building regulations if you are adding a new bathroom, for example converting a former bedroom into a new bathroom.

What are your payment terms?

We request that 50% of the overall bill is paid upfront and the remaining 50% is paid upon completion of the project, once you are happy with your new bathroom.
